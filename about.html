Introduction

Hi, I’m Shiyue Liu, but you can call me Lingo. In this collection, I’m excited to share some of the visual art projects I’ve created from high school to college, including posters, videos, and media research. My work is divided into two categories: hobbies and specialties. Many of my projects reflect my deep interest in traditional Chinese culture and capturing everyday life, themes that you’ll notice throughout this collection. Additionally, as a media major in college, I’ve conducted extensive research on social media, producing academic articles and creating self-designed advertisements. Now, I invite you to step into Lingo’s world and explore my journey.
