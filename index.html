About Me
Hello, I’m Shiyue Liu, also known as Lingo. As an integrated media and technology designer, I specialize in crafting compelling visual narratives and innovative solutions that bridge the gap between art, technology, and communication. This portfolio showcases a curated selection of my work, organized into two categories: personal explorations and professional specializations. These projects reflects my deep appreciation for traditional Chinese culture and daily life feelings. From graphic design and immersive videos to strategic media campaigns and data-driven research, my work demonstrates a multidisciplinary approach that combines creativity with technical precision. With expertise in social media analysis, I’ve conducted in-depth research on its role in shaping modern communication strategies, producing both academic insights and practical applications through targeted advertisements and content design. I invite you to explore this collection and discover how I leverage integrated media and technology to create impactful, meaningful experiences across diverse platforms.

UI/UX

This section presents the design of a museum-focused app, organized into several modules. Its main functions include museum introductions, cultural relics categorized by pavilion, ticket booking, content explanations, map navigation, highlights of the museum's collection, online purchasing of cultural and creative products, and customer service. The design addresses common challenges faced by visitors, such as the absence of on-site staff when assistance is needed. By incorporating these features, the app goes beyond traditional explanation-based functionalities, offering a more comprehensive and immersive museum experience.
