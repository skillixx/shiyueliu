Video Group 2: Exciting Scenes

This video about the city that never sleeps in the Tang Dynasty belongs to a video promoting the culture of the Tang Dynasty. The reason why I want to do it is that I like Chinese traditional culture very much and hope that more people can know about it. Besides, my hometown was the capital of the Tang Dynasty at that time, so it brought me great convenience. The classical buildings in the video all still exist after thousands of years. The songs and dances in the video also tell the development process of the Tang Dynasty, and the classical costumes, ornaments and food of the Tang Dynasty displayed by me all show a long history.
